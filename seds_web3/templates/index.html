Vision

SEDS KCT is a student organisation which promotes exploration and development of Space. We stalk on this mission by supporting a network of interested people and develop their Leadership and also involving them in Space related projects.
We nourish our members through constant encouragements, supporting in their endeavors and also create a platform to reach higher altitudes.

About Us

SEDS is an independent non- profit, student based organisation which promotes the exploration and development of space.The prime initiative is to generate and stimulate interest amongst its members in space sciences and promotion of hobbies of astronomy, astrophysics, stargazing has taken a good stand. SEDS nurtures its member's innovativeness and makes them aware with the latest in space technology. SEDS thrives to provide avenues for career opportunities and to reveal opportunities for young engineers and students hailed from various fields.SEDS KCT is one of the chapters of SEDS INDIA.
